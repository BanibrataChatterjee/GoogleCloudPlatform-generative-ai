 The table shows the premium discount rates for different policy durations.

- For a policy duration of 4 months, the premium discount is 50%.
- For a policy duration of 5 months, the premium discount is 60%.
- For a policy duration of 6 months, the premium discount is 70%.
- For a policy duration of 7 months, the premium discount is 75%.
- For a policy duration of 8 months, the premium discount is 80%.
- For a policy duration of 9 months, the premium discount is 85%.
- For a policy duration exceeding 9 months, there is no discount and the full annual premium is charged.
