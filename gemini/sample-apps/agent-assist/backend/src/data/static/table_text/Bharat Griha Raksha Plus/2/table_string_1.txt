 The table is about insurance coverage for physical loss or damage to property.

Column A lists the perils that are covered under the insurance policy. These perils include:

1. Fire caused by burning of Insured Property by order of any Public Authority
2. Explosion or Implosion
3. Lightning
4. Earthquake, volcanic eruption, or other convulsions of nature
5. Storm, Cyclone, Typhoon, Tempest, Hurricane, Tornado, Tsunami, Flood and Inundation

Column B lists the perils that are not covered under the insurance policy. These perils include:

1. Any loss or damage, or destruction caused to the Insured Property by physical loss or damage, or destruction caused to the Insured Property
